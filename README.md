THINGS I LEARNT
CYBERSECURITY: Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer system, networks, devices and date from unauthorized access, theft, digital attacks and damages.

CYBERSECURITY JOB:
SOC ANALYST: Soc analyst stands for Security Operation Center. They do the defending job in Cybersecurity.

Penetration Tester: They find vulnerabilities in systems using authorised access and report them. Penetration testers are also known as Ethical Hackers.
Types include, 
White hat – they get permission first before launching any attack.
Black hat – they target victims for financial gain and operate illegally.
Grey hat – they may act without permission, but not always with bad intentions, depending on the person.

Digital Forensic: They investigate cyber incident after they occur by collecting digitak evidence.

Security Engineers: They build and secures systems, set up firewalls, IDs and security control.
Firewall: is a security tool that is used to monitor both incoming and outgoing traffic.
IDs: Intrusion detection system. it is a system set up that detects suspicious traffic and alert the system or analyst.

Red Teamer: They simulate real-world attacks to identify vulnerabilities and exploit them, helping organisations understand how attackers think and improve their security.
